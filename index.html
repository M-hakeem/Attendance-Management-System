About QRATT

QR code attendance system records attendance of students through the use of QR Code. It allows Lecturer to log in and generate the QR Code which will be displayed for students at the beginning and end of every lecture, for students to scan in and out using their phone camera to confirm their attendance. The whole process takes less than a minute for any student as well for the whole class to complete their attendance confirmation, The Code contains the Course title, Lecturer Name, Date/Time and Venue. So also, the system includes the student ID, time and date of the scan when saving the information. It also saves information locally when there’s an internet connection and saves it to the server when connected to the internet.
